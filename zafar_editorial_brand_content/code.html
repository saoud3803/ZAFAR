We reject the superfluous. Our approach is rooted in the honesty of materials and the severity of form. Each garment is a structure, engineered for permanence in an ephemeral world.

ZAFAR emerges from the collision of architectural brutalism and high fashion. We strip away the unnecessary to reveal the essential core of modern luxury. Our atelier in Milan is dedicated to uncompromising craftsmanship, utilizing heavyweight textiles and rigid tailoring to create silhouettes that command space rather than inhabit it.
